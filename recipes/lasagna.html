The perfect Lasagna

Everybody loves a good lasagne. But a good lasagne is not only a lasagne. You have to put in the best ingredients to make your loved ones happy. This is how you succeed.
  • 150 g bacoon or pancetta
  • 2 ss olje
  • 400 g minced roedeer meat
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 2 boxes of crushed tomatos
  • Oregano
First you need to fry the meat and onions. Then you put all the ingrediences in a pan. Put in the oven and let it roast in the owen for 45 minutes.
  1. Set the oven on 200 celcius
  2. Fry the meat and onion
  3. Put the pan in the oven
  4. Recieve appreciation
